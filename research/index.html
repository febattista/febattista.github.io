research

The methodologies in my research span several sub-fields of optimization with the following main thrusts: bilevel, multi-stage stochastic and multiobjective mixed-integer linear problems; and graph optimization problems. My goal as a researcher is to bridge the gap between theory and computation by developing open-source software.
